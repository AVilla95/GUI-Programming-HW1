Final Verse

Story Abilities Noctua Final Verse

After Dante unleashes his mighty spells through both his Light Verse and Dark Verse abilities, he can use their combined power to unleash an ultimate ability called Final Verse. Depending on the combination of abilities he's stocked up, they can create 1 of 4 different effects.

◯ = Light Verse   ✕ = Dark Verse

◯ + ◯

Triumph

 Grants a buff to self and allies that increases Health and gives Overguard. Kills and assists during the buff duration also grant additional Overguard.
✕ + ✕

Tragedy

 Casting this ability causes any DOT (Damage Over Time) effects planted on enemies from Slash, Heat, and Toxin to immediately resolve. Causing all future damage to happen instantaneously.
◯ + ✕

Wordwarden

 Gives allies ephemeral copies of his Noctua book to aid them in their attacks.
✕ + ◯

Pageflight

 Summons Paragrimm birds to attack and distract enemies. Affected enemies that were hits by one of these birds gain a vulnerability to status attacks.
