Noctua Light Verse
Open Noctua, Dante’s Exalted Tome, and unleash a tale of woe upon his enemies. Dante’s vitalizing composition grants him and his allies Overguard and increases their Max Health.

Dante’s composition draws blood from nearby enemies, inflicting Slash Damage upon them. Dante must compose two other Verses before his Final Verse.
